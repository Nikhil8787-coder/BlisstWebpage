Our Mission

Blisst is here to redefine aging in India by blending care, companionship, and adventure into one platform. We connect elderly individuals with support and youth with purpose—creating a stronger, happier community.

Whether it's a helping hand at home, a friend to share a laugh with, or a safe way to explore the world, Blisst brings bliss to every doorstep.

Our Services

Helper Services

Domestic Help: Cooking, cleaning, caretaking, and medical support tailored to your needs.

SoulTalk: A companion to chat, share stories, or spend time with—perfect for those living alone or with enochlophobia.

Tours & Travel

Package Tours: Pre-planned trips with itineraries for ease.

Customized Tours: Build your dream trip within your budget, with a dedicated assistant for comfort.

Emergency Alert

One-tap access to contact loved ones or nearby police stations for instant help in emergencies.

Intercity Bus Booking

Book seats with ease. Groups of 5+ get an assistant, or opt for private support from boarding to arrival.

Community Events

Stay updated on religious Activities , yoga , laughter classes and physiotherapy classes.
